Upcoming Member Events

Annual Harvest Sale

On the third Saturday of September we host our members only Annual Harvest Sale. Sign up to sell your produce or come on down and shop the freshest organic produce in the city. Sale starts at 9:00 am sharp at our club greenhouse and we recommend bringing a wagon. Sign in for more details or to register as a seller.

Workshops

We host quarterly gardening workshops for all ages on a variety of topics. This quarter we are hosting a youth planting event at our club greenhouse. Children of all ages will learn how to plant seeds and how to care for their plants. There will be a variety of fruit, vegetable and flowering plants to choose from. We encourage all parents to sign their children up today. Sign in for more information or to sign up for a workshop.

Garden Tool Exchange

Members can come on down to the club greenhouse anytime during open hours to borrow, trade or donate garden tools. Our garden tool exchange program is quite robust and you will most likely find what you are looking for. We also use these tools for our quarterly workshops and donations are gladly accepted. Sign in for more details.
